Why us?

We are not salesmen - we actively work on projects. Our approach is to get technical as early as possible, in order to save time and avoid misunderstandings. Our immediate area of expertise is mobile and cloud, and we maintain contacts with talented developers of various specialities in Poland, so we can scale if needed.

We love mobile!

We don't just make apps. We develop them according to the best practices and modern standards, and if necessary, help you cut corners without sacrificing your product’s future.

  • Kotlin since 2015
  • Responsive user interfaces that support various screen sizes (smartphones, tablets, TV...)
  • Optimizing apps for threading, battery use, memory consumption
  • Efficient networking with backend optimized for mobile networks
  • Ready for international markets with proper l10n and g11n
  • Designing public API and SDK, version control, app variants and white label experience
  • Delivering high quality apps with tools and processes like CI, CD, TDD, monitoring and analytics

A Complete solution.

We take care of it all, should you require so. Our engineers have acquired domain specific knowledge, and continuous growth is at the core of our philosophy. We can deliver backend for your app too, and deploy it to the cloud of your choice.

  • Design and implement public API and SDK
  • Highly scalable, distributed and fault tolerant backend using microservices architecture
  • Full stack development to deliver complete backend solution
  • Deploy to AWS, Google Cloud, Azure or elsewhere
  • Use transactional and NoSQL databases depending on your needs
  • We can optimize existing systems to improve performance and make them more cost efficient
  • Would you like us to support or develop your legacy application? We can help too

Agile + reliable
