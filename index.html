About

We are a reliable technology partner specialising in our two flagship services: extended teams, and tandems. We are headquartered in Warsaw, Poland, and are focusing in catering to software development needs of companies based in Sweden, Norway and Denmark.

What are Extended Teams?

An extended development team is, as the name suggests, an extension of your core team. There are several characteristics of an extended team that make it an attractive option for your business:

  • An extended team augments your core team, it does not replace it. The key business and technical competency is still protected, while the extension team is setup to bridge the skill gaps in your core staff.
  • You have full control over the project. The constant direct communication between the in-house staff and the extended team developers makes it all function as one unit.
  • The responsibilities are distributed among all team members, both core and extended. All of them are equally invested into the project.

What are Tandems?

Tandems is our innovative modification of the extended team concept. A tandem is a pair of two developers: a junior one, who is participating in your project full time, and a senior one, who is available part time, and is mentoring the junior developer. There are many benefits of this setup:

  • It's cost effective. Two developers join your team, yet your cost is significantly lower than the cost of two engineers.
  • You get to benefit from a senior developer's experience. Available for your project at least one day a week, he or she will provide valuable input to your team's competence and boost its effectiveness, at a fraction of cost.
  • The junior developer is mentored by the senior. There is no better way to learn and grow than to be mentored by someone experienced. The junior developer will consult the senior in case of solving complex problems, which otherwise could take a lot of time. Additionally, his or hers competence will be regularly expanded through a series of one-to-one workshops, technically relevant to your business focus.
  • The tandem communicates well. Since they cooperate closely on regular basis, and spend time together during the workshops and mentoring, their interactions are more effective. They simply know each other better, and you get a great culture fit, at no cost.
